This is us

We are a team of dedicated artists, programmers and designers, working closely with our customers to create stunning visual & interactive experiences. Based directly south of Odense, in the middle of Denmark, our team has built a reputation for good quality & unique solutions.

We share a passion for game development and that is our drive towards making gamesolutions in virtual reality.

Virtual reality has shown to be resourceful and extremely effective when it comes to communication and immersion. Our team stand together to develop a finished product that covers all aspects of our skills in graphics, programming, gamedesign and more.
