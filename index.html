VR and Healthcare


There are countless ways for VR to improve and deliver possibilities for health care. With VR, health services, such as hospitals and clinics, will be able to train students and doctors with gamesolutions designed by our team. Our team works in close collaboration with you to achieve the exact desired product.


-VR for training students and doctors

-VR as therapy for patients suffering from anxiety, phobias and more

-VR for healing patients with entertainment


With VR-glasses you can enter another world close to reality, where it’s not just about entertainment, but also about learning.
